Inspiration

This project was inspired from a few different games below we share what inspired us


Flappy bird

Flappy bird took the world by storm back in early 2014 after having been developed a short time before.

Users from around the world became hooked trying to get through an endless array of pipes.

Frustrated critics were harsh on with the games reviews but, now it will forever be a moment in gaming history.



Altos Adventure

Altos Adventure is a beautiful minimalist endless snowboarding game

Altos beautiful procedurally generated landscape's captured the attention of many people back in 2015 when released,

its simplistic yet effective use of shape and design helps immerse the player within the game.
